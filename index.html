Frontend

Frontend design is the art and science of creating visually engaging and intuitive user interfaces for websites and applications. It involves crafting the look and feel of a digital product, ensuring a seamless and enjoyable experience for users. Frontend designers use a combination of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to bring designs to life, focusing on responsiveness and cross-browser compatibility.

Backend

Backend design is the architectural backbone of web applications, responsible for the server-side logic and data management that powers the functionality of websites and applications. Backend designers, often known as backend developers, design and implement the server-side code, databases, and APIs that enable seamless communication between the frontend and the server.
